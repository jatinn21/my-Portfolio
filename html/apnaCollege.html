ApnaCollege is an e-learning system that was built as a final year project for BCA in 2022. It is a full-stack system that was designed and developed using HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, and MySQL. The project was created with a lot of passion and dedication, which earned the creator an impressive score of 84% on their final year.

The system offers a range of features for students, including the ability to register, purchase courses, change their profile picture/password because let's be real, we all love a good profile pic change. Students can also provide feedback on the courses they have taken. Students can explore a range of courses and purchase them with ease, thanks to the brilliant integration of Razorpay as the payment gateway. They can also view the courses provided by the admin and see the feedback given by other students before making a purchase.

And, if you're wondering who's the boss here, it's the admin, of course! The admin has the power to add/delete students and courses, view lessons within each course, and even delete them if necessary. Although ApnaCollege is not currently live, it remains a testament to my skills and dedication as a developer.
